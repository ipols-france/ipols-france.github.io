About the meeting

The 2017 Annual Meeting of the GDRI Physics of Living Systems (iPoLS) was held at the Institut Pierre-Gilles de Gennes (IPGG), 6 Rue Jean Calvin, Paris. The IPGG — named after the Nobel laureate physicist Pierre-Gilles de Gennes — is a research centre at the crossroads of microfluidics, biology, and physics, housing teams from Institut Curie, ENS, ESPCI Paris, and Chimie ParisTech.

The meeting brought together the international Physics of Living Systems community for four days of talks, poster sessions, and discussions at the physics–biology interface. The social programme included a dinner cruise on the Seine river (Bateaux Mouches, Port de la Conférence).
