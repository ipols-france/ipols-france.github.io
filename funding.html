Funding Applications

Support schemes for early-career researchers

iPoLS France supports early-career researchers through two funding schemes. Applications are reviewed by the coordination committee.

1. iPoLS Annual Meeting Support

For PhD students and postdocs attending the annual international iPoLS meeting (registration fees and/or travel support).

Applications are evaluated in two waves: end of April and end of May 2026. We encourage early submission.


Apply for Meeting Support →

2. Mobility Grant

For PhD students and postdocs visiting a partner laboratory in the iPoLS network (duration: 1–4 weeks).

The application form will be available online soon.


Apply for a Mobility Grant →
Applications are collected via online forms and reviewed by the coordination committee. For any questions, please use the Contact page.
